Project Summary

I developed a professional corporate website for Lava, a company specializing in construction and contracting.The website, built with WordPress , showcases the company’s services and projects in a structured and engaging manner.

Website Sections:

  • 🏠 Home Page: A well-designed landing page providing an overview of the company with an intuitive navigation experience.
  • 🔧 Our Services: A dedicated section detailing the company's construction and contracting services in a clear and organized layout.
  • 🏗 Projects: A portfolio-style section displaying Lava’s key projects with images and descriptions that highlight quality and professionalism.
  • 📞 Contact Us: A user-friendly contact form for easy communication with the company for inquiries or service requests.

Features:

  • ✅ Modern & Responsive Design– Fully optimized for all screen sizes and devices.
  • ✅ Well-Structured Content – Organized information for easy browsing and readability.
  • ✅ Dedicated Sections – Separate pages for services, projects, and company details.
  • ✅ Integrated Contact Form– Simplifies customer communication and inquiries.
  • ✅ Ongoing Support & Maintenance– Ensured updates and performance optimization.

The goal of this project was to create a visually appealing, easy-to-navigate website that enhances Lava’s online presence and facilitates better engagement with clients and future projects.

Skills and Technologies Used

Platform: WordPress
💻 Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
🛠 Backend: PHP (WordPress CMS)
🔌 Plugins: Elementor, Contact Form 7, SEO optimization tools
