Project Summary

I developed a professional website for marble and stone manufacturing factory using WordPress The website includes essential pages that showcase the factory’s products, services, and projects in a structured and visually appealing manner. The goal was to enhance the factory's online presence and attract potential customers through a modern, user-friendly design

Website Sections:

  • 🏠 Home Page: A well-designed interface reflecting the factory’s identity and showcasing key products.
  • 📞 Contact Us: A contact form including all essential details for easy customer communication.
  • 🏗 Projects: A portfolio displaying previous projects with images and detailed descriptions.
  • 👥 Our Team: A section introducing the team members with their roles and images.
  • 🛍 Products: A comprehensive catalog displaying available products with images and pricing details.
  • 🏢 Industries: Information on the different sectors the factory serves, such as construction and interior design.

Features:

  • ✅ 6 fully customized pages with a modern and structured layout.
  • 📱 Responsive design for seamless experience across all devices (mobile, tablet, desktop).
  • 🔒 Security enhancements using specialized plugins for data protection.
  • ⚡ Optimized performance with caching and SEO improvements.
  • 📝 Admin-friendly content management with easy update options.

The goal of this project was to create a visually appealing, easy-to-navigate website that enhances Lava’s online presence and facilitates better engagement with clients and future projects.

Skills and Technologies Used

🖥 Platform: WordPress
💻 Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
🛠 Backend: PHP (WordPress CMS)
🔌 Plugins: Elementor, Contact Form 7, SEO optimization tools
